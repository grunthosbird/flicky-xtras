I have developed two versions for this test. The first - featuring a tag search - uses jQuery, Bootstrap 4, and Angular. The second - featuring infinite scrolling - is pure/vanilla Javascript, CSS, and HTML.

BOOTSTRAP & ANGULAR
- tag search

A modern SPA that uses Bootstrap 4. It can speed up development dramatically, provide excellent cross browser compatability, and aid the lesser-skilled with their design. Angular, which is often great but has its critics, at the right time can be a massive help - but the more unconventional your task, the more likely you are to find issues.

VANILLA
- infinite scrolling

Vanilla - stripped back, library and framework free, version to demonstrate my knowledge from the ground up. It shows that, fantastic as these libraries are, one need not always be entirely reliant on them. I have adopted the revealing module pattern, which provides excellent encapsulation and thus encourages further separation of concerns.
